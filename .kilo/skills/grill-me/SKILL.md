# grill-me

Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching a shared understanding of the problem and solution. Walk through each branch of the decision tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one by one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time.

If a question can not be answered, explore the codebase to find the answer. If the codebase is not available, explore your memory bank to find the answer.

## Steps

1. Follow your custom instructions
2. Explore the codebase 
3. Expore the provided plan or design
4. Ask the user questions
5. For each question, provide your recommended answer
6. Continue asking questions until all branches of the decision tree are resolved and a shared understanding of the problem and solution is reached.
